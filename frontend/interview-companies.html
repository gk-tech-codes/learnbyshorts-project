About Tech Interview Preparation

Technical interviews at top tech companies can be challenging, but with the right preparation and understanding of common patterns, you can succeed. Our story-based approach helps you learn not just the solutions, but the thought process behind solving complex problems under pressure.

Tech interviews typically consist of several rounds:

  • Phone/Video Screen: Initial technical screening with coding questions and basic problem-solving.
  • Technical Rounds: Deep dive into algorithms, data structures, and coding challenges.
  • System Design: Architecture and scalability discussions for senior positions.
  • Behavioral Rounds: Culture fit, leadership principles, and past experience discussions.

Each company has its unique interview style and focus areas. We provide real interview experiences through engaging stories that help you understand the interviewer's perspective and learn effective problem-solving approaches.
